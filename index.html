Our Story

Paw Pets Foundation, based in Palghar, is a dedicated NGO that has been improving the quality of life for sick and ailing dogs and cats for the past 20 years. Through the unwavering dedication of our volunteers and the generous support of our donors, we have established a shelter where these animals receive the care they need to become self-reliant.

Our efforts focus on feeding, sterilization, surgeries, and vaccination programs, ensuring each furry friend gets a chance at a healthier, happier life. Our long-standing commitment to this cause is a testament to our mission to touch and transform the lives of as many animals as possible.

Mission-Pawsible

At Paw Pets Foundation, our mission is to improve the quality of life for sick and ailing dogs and cats. For over 20 years, we have been dedicated to providing comprehensive care that includes feeding, sterilization, surgeries, and vaccinations. Our goal is to create a safe and nurturing environment where these animals can recover and thrive, becoming self-reliant and ready for a brighter future. Through the tireless efforts of our volunteers and the generous support of our donors, we strive to touch and transform the lives of as many furry friends as possible, ensuring they receive the love and care they deserve.
