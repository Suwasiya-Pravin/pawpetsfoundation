Our Story

Paw Pets Foundation was established with a vision to make a difference in the lives of stray animals. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to rescuing, feeding, sheltering, and finding forever homes for stray animals.

Our journey began with a small group of passionate animal lovers who decided to take action to address the growing issue of stray animals in our community. We started by organizing rescue missions, providing food and shelter to abandoned animals, and offering medical care to those in need.

Mission-Pawsible

Paw Pets Foundation's mission is to rescue, feed, shelter, and adopt stray animals. We provide vaccinations, neutering, and medical treatment for injured animals. Our goal is to give dignified last rites to all strays and find good homes for adoption of deserted animals.
